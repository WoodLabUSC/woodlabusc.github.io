Welcome to the research group of John C. Wood at Children's Hospital Los Angeles (CHLA).

Our current research focuses on in vivo measurement of iron using magnetic resonance imaging (MRI).

We are located in the Division of Cardiology, Department of Radiology and Saban Research Institute at CHLA.
